Visit Us Today

Rescue Relax Repeat Coffee & Cats Await

We blend the joy of freshly brewed coffee with the magic of feline companionship. As a rescue-based cat café, every visit helps. Whether you're here to unwind or make a new furry friend, you’re part of something special.

More Than a Café

We partner with local rescues to provide a safe, playful space for adoptable cats. Each visit supports our mission, funding food, medical care, and shelter for these furry friends while they wait for their forever families.
